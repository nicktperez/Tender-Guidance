Partners often feel helpless. Give them a job.

## During Labor
*   **Gatekeeper:** Managing the doctors/nurses/family. protecting the vibe.
*   **Hydration Manager:** Offering water between every contraction.
*   **DJ:** controlling the music/lighting.

## Postpartum (The Real Work)
*   **Vistor Bouncer:** "It's time for [Name] to rest now, thanks for coming."
*   **Night Shift:** Taking the baby for a 4-hour stretch so you can get uninterrupted sleep.
*   **Food Logistics:** ensuring you have a steady stream of snacks while feeding the baby.
