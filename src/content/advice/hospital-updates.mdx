"Has it started?" "Any news?" "How many centimeters?"

The phone blowing up during labor is a major distraction.

## The Strategy: The Point Person
Designate a text-captain (partner, doula, or trusted friend).
"We will be turning our phones off to focus. [Name] will send an update when there is news to share. No news = we are busy!"
